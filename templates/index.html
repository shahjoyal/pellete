Abhitech's Flagship Product

OB-1205
Organic Binder

A precision-engineered organic binder that activates through moisture in raw mix, forming an adhesive film that holds particles together during the balling stage — reducing bentonite dependence and improving pellet integrity.

💧

Moisture-Activated Performance

OB-1205 activates using moisture in the raw mix, absorbing excess moisture to form a robust adhesive film on particle surfaces — controlling the critical 9.5–10.5% moisture window.

🔗

Reduced Bentonite Consumption

Enables significant step-wise reduction in bentonite dosage while maintaining or improving green ball characteristics — drop number (GCS) and pellet integrity.

🔥

Crack & Fine Reduction

Increases pellet integrity during induration, reducing cracked pellets, fine generation and joint pellet formation in both straight grate and travelling grate-kiln systems.

🏗️

Grade & Blend Adaptability

OB dosage can be optimized for both high-grade (61–63% Fe) and low-grade (58–60% Fe) iron ores and any blending composition.

Process Parameters

Key Parameters OB-1205 Controls

OB-1205 dosage is optimized against each of these critical process variables to maximize pellet quality and production efficiency.

🪨
Iron Ore Grade

High grade (61–63% Fe) and low grade (58–60% Fe) require different OB dosages. Step-wise optimization is critical during grade transitions.

Fe: 58–63% | Al₂O₃ <3–4%
🔀
Blend Composition

Blending of different iron ore grades impacts recycle rate. OB dosage is increased during blend changes to prevent collapsing pellets.

Blend: 70:30 to 30:70 ratio
💧
Filter Cake Moisture

Optimum moisture 9.5–10.5%. OB controls moisture behavior to prevent undersize or lump formation.

Optimum: 9.5% – 10.5%
📐
Blain Number

Surface area parameter (1800–2400 cm²/g) governing agglomeration. OB dosage compensates for variations in fineness.

Target: 1800–2400 cm²/g
🧹
Fines Generation

Fines reduce permeability and draft pressure causing improper heat transfer. OB minimizes fines in both straight grate and kiln systems.

Straight Grate | TG-Kiln
⚡
Bulk Density & PSD

OB promotes uniform size distribution across all pellet size fractions (8–10, 10–12, 12–14, 14–16mm), improving bulk density consistency.

PSD: 8mm – 16mm
